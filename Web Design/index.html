About SDG 16

Goal 16 is about promoting peaceful and inclusive societies, providing access to justice and developing accountable and inclusive institutions at every level. It states that sustainable development cannot be achieved in places without peace, security and protection of human rights and strong government systems.

SDG 16 describes issues such as violence, corruption, lack of transparency, weak institutions, and inclusive access to justice. Strong government institutions are essential for providing public services, protecting human rights, and ensuring accountability.

Introduction to the Proposal

The proposed International Accountability and Justice Platform (IAJP) refers to institutional multilayered breakdown through structured, technological accountability mechanisms. Its goal is to ease the process of gathering and documenting evidence of the war crimes and crimes that are committed as a result of the war.
